Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to establish an online presence for 'Life Adventure', a rafting business. The website will provide information about our services, trip details, safety protocols, and allow customers to book trips online. This will help us reach a wider audience and enhance customer engagement.

Audience

The target audience for our rafting website includes adventure enthusiasts, families looking for recreational activities, and corporate groups seeking team-building experiences. Here are three scenario questions to understand our audience better:
1. Are you an adventure enthusiast looking for your next thrilling experience?
2. Are you a family planning a fun and adventurous outing?
3. Are you a corporate team looking for an exciting team-building activity?
